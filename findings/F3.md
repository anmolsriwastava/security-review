# Formula Sheets Accessible to Free Accounts

**Severity:** High-Medium 
**Endpoint:** /dosts/formula-sheet/[id]

## Description

Formula sheet pages are accessible to free trial accounts via direct URL.
The UI hides these from free users by sending a pop up on the page 
but the page loads when accessed directly. No plan upgrade prompt or courses page appears.

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Log into a free student account
2. Visit ( for e.g. /dosts/formula-sheet/6a3e4f02d36a314aa05d7f5f ) directly
3. Formula sheet loads and is fully readable

## PoC

- /dosts/formula-sheet/6a3e4f02d36a314aa05d7f5f opened with free account.
- Content was fully accessible by free account.
- Blocked for unauthenticated users.

## Impact

Free users can access paid study material by obtaining direct URLs from
paid users and easily circulate it on telegram, reddit etc.
