# Paid Video Content Accessible Without Authentication

**Severity:** Critical
**Endpoint:** /video/subconcept/[id]     (e.g. /video/subconcept/102282XX)

## Description

Paid video lectures play without any login or payment. Visiting a video
URL directly loads the full content. No authentication check on the video
page. Works in private windows, incognito, and different browsers with no
account logged in.

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Get any regular video URL (like /video/subconcept/509910861) from a paid account. 
2. Open private window or incognito, no login is needed there. 
3. Paste the URL.
4. Video plays. No login or payment asked.

## PoC

Tested and confirmed working without auth:
- /video/subconcept/509910861
- /video/subconcept/532111263
- /video/subconcept/532122971

All played in private browser windows with no account logged in.

## Impact

Anyone with a video URL can watch paid content. URLs can be shared on
Telegram, WhatsApp, social media. Complete bypass of payment and access
controls. Direct revenue loss.
